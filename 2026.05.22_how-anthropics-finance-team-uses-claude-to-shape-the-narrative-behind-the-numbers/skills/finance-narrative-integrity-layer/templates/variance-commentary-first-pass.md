# Monthly variance commentary (first pass)

## Prompt
Write the first pass of monthly variance commentary in the voice we already use.

Inputs:
- This month’s table: <paste or attach>
- Baseline/forecast table: <paste or attach>
- Prior-month commentary (for voice and conventions): <paste>
- Any supporting context links: <paste>

Voice + structure constraints:
- Follow this pattern: “Revenue was A versus B, off by C%, driven by D.”
- Keep tone consistent with the prior month.
- Prefer short paragraphs and explicit drivers.

Deliverable:
- A ready-to-edit narrative section with 3–7 bullets of key drivers, plus a short paragraph summary.

# Monthly financial review workflow checklist

1) Open the monthly review doc.
2) Confirm it is structured as variance analysis versus forecast.
3) Drop the relevant financial table into the doc.
4) Add links to supporting context.
5) Draft the first pass using: [../templates/variance-commentary-first-pass.md](../templates/variance-commentary-first-pass.md)
6) Edit for judgment, nuance, and stakeholder alignment.
7) Preserve the established voice month over month.

# New model first-look prompts

## Prompt: summarize key drivers
Summarize the key drivers in this model.
- What are the main outputs and how are they calculated?
- What assumptions most strongly move the outputs?
- Which tabs/sections should I read first?

## Prompt: flag structural issues
Before I invest time in this model, flag potential structural issues.
- Any circular references, inconsistent time granularities, or duplicated calculations?
- Any places where totals don’t foot, or where definitions appear inconsistent across tabs?
- Any outputs that don’t clearly tie back to inputs?

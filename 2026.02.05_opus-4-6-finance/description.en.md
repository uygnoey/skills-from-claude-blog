## What is this post?
This post announces Claude Opus 4.6 for finance work, highlighting improved multi-step financial reasoning and more polished first-pass deliverables, plus product updates for Cowork, Claude in Excel, and a research preview of Claude in PowerPoint.

## When is it useful?
- When you need help with complex financial analysis and want a cleaner, more structured first draft.
- When your workflow lives in spreadsheets, slide decks, and desktop files, and you want the model to operate directly in those formats.

## Key points
- Opus 4.6 is positioned as improving accuracy and focus on long, multi-step finance tasks (with human review still required).
- Cowork supports running multiple analyses at once and can be customized with plugins (bundles of skills plus connectors).
- Claude in Excel adds stronger spreadsheet editing abilities and quality-of-life features like auto-compaction and drag-and-drop multi-file support.
- Claude in PowerPoint (research preview) can edit existing slides and generate first-pass decks aligned with existing templates.

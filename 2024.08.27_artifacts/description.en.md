## What is this post?
This post announces that Artifacts are generally available across Claude.ai plans and can be created/viewed on mobile apps.

## When is it useful?
Use this when you want a dedicated workspace inside a Claude conversation to iterate on things like code, documents, and visualizations (including interactive artifacts such as websites or dashboards).

## Key points
- Artifacts are available on Free, Pro, and Team plans.
- Artifacts can be created and viewed on iOS and Android.
- Artifacts provide a dedicated space to build and iterate on code, documents, and visualizations.
- Free and Pro users can publish and remix Artifacts.
- Team users can share Artifacts in Projects and collaborate in a secure environment.

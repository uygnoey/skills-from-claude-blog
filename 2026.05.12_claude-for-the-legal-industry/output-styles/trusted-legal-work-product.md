# Trusted legal work product

Use this output style when generating legal work product where trust, grounding, and verification matter.

## Principles
- Ground claims in authoritative materials provided by the user or reputable primary sources.
- Avoid overconfidence; state assumptions and uncertainty.
- Make the escalation path explicit for high-risk issues.
- Prefer clear, business-readable summaries alongside legal detail.

## Required sections
- **What you reviewed** (documents, playbooks, authoritative sources)
- **Findings** (key issues, prioritized)
- **Recommended actions** (including fallback language locations when applicable)
- **Open questions / missing info**
- **Escalation recommendation** (who, when, why)
- **Delivery-ready checks** (formatting, comment scrubbing, final review)

# Claude for the legal industry

## What is this post?
Anthropic announces new capabilities for legal workflows: 20+ MCP connectors for common legal software and 12 practice-area plugins in the Legal Marketplace.

## When is it useful?
- When legal teams need Claude to work across the tools where legal work happens (documents, email, matters, research, discovery, and data rooms).
- When a team wants reusable, practice-specific workflows that start from a short setup interview (playbook, escalation chain, risk calibration, and house style).

## Key points
- MCP connectors bring legal work into Claude across the legal technology stack.
- Practice-area plugins package common workflows for roles like Commercial Legal, Privacy Legal, Litigation Legal, etc.
- Both connectors and plugins use open protocols so teams can customize to their practice.
- Claude is positioned to support multi-document, end-to-end workflows (e.g., redlining in Word, cover notes in Outlook, checklists in Excel, summaries in PowerPoint).

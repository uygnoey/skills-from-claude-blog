# Workflow checklists (legal)

These checklists are derived from the workflows described in the source post.

## Contract drafting / redlining
- Confirm governing law, parties, and transaction structure.
- Compare clauses against the team playbook (clause-by-clause).
- Track every change and explain rationale for each change.
- Pull fallback language from approved playbooks.
- Prepare a business-readable summary of key issues.
- Scrub internal comments before sending to the counterparty.
- Run a final formatting check on the execution copy.

## Matter intake triage
- Triage incoming matter work and identify the request type.
- Identify missing information required to proceed.
- Flag urgent deadlines and propose follow-ups.
- Classify the matter by risk/priority.
- Recommend escalation per the escalation chain.

## Regulatory update sweep
- Watch for regulatory updates in relevant jurisdictions.
- Filter updates to the agreed materiality threshold.
- Compare new rules against the policy/library of precedents.
- Track gaps and comment deadlines.
- Draft a board or leadership note when needed.

## Product launch review
- Collect feature description, target users, and go-to-market claims.
- Check marketing claims for legal risk.
- Identify privacy/data protection touchpoints.
- Summarize launch risks and open questions.
- Recommend escalation or approval conditions.

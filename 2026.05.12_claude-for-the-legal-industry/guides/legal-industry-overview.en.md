# Legal industry overview: connectors + practice-area plugins

## What is this post?
This post announces new capabilities for legal teams: 20+ MCP connectors for legal software and 12 practice-area plugins for common legal workflows.

## When is it useful?
Use this guide when you want to understand how to structure a legal deployment of Claude:
- what to connect (connectors)
- what to standardize (practice-area plugins)
- what to customize (your playbooks, precedents, and escalation process)

## Key points
- **Connectors** bring content from the legal technology stack into Claude.
- **Practice-area plugins** package repeatable workflows for common legal roles.
- Plugins start with a **short setup interview** to learn playbooks, escalation chain, risk calibration, and house style.

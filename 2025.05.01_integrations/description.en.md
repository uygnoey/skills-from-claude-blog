# Claude can now connect to your world

## What is this post?

The launch announcement for **Integrations** — Claude's way of connecting to apps and tools through remote MCP servers — and the expansion of **advanced Research**. Together they let Claude pull context from your work apps, search the web and Google Workspace, and produce comprehensive cited reports in five to forty-five minutes. Originally published 2025-05-01; an update on 2025-06-03 expanded availability to Pro and made web search global on all paid plans.

## When useful

- Deciding whether and how to connect a third-party app to Claude via Integrations rather than building bespoke automation.
- Planning how to use advanced Research for multi-source investigations that would otherwise take hours of manual work.
- Onboarding a team to the Integrations + Research workflow and standardizing which apps to connect first.
- Looking up which services were available in the launch lineup and which were on the roadmap.

## Key points

- Integrations connect Claude to **remote MCP servers** across web and desktop, extending the November 2024 MCP launch beyond local servers in Claude Desktop.
- Launch lineup of 10 services: **Atlassian Jira, Atlassian Confluence, Zapier, Cloudflare, Intercom, Asana, Square, Sentry, PayPal, Linear, Plaid**. Roadmap mentions **Stripe, GitLab, Box**.
- Developers can build their own Integrations in as little as 30 minutes; Cloudflare provides built-in OAuth, transport handling, and deployment.
- Concrete patterns called out: Zapier connects thousands of apps via pre-built workflows (e.g. pull HubSpot sales data, prepare meeting briefs from calendar); Atlassian for collaborative product planning and bulk Confluence/Jira updates; Intercom's AI agent **Fin** acts as an MCP client and can file Linear bugs from user reports.
- Advanced Research breaks a request into sub-investigations across hundreds of sources, returns a cited report in **5–45 minutes**, and now searches the web, Google Workspace, **and** any connected Integration.
- **Availability**: at launch, Integrations and advanced Research were beta on Max, Team, Enterprise; web search global on paid plans. After 2025-06-03 update, Integrations and Research available on Pro, Max, Team, Enterprise; web search global on all Claude plans.

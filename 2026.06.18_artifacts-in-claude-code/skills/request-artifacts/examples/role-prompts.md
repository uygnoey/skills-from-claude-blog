# Role-based example prompts

These prompts are quoted from the source blog post.

- Legal / open source: "Build an artifact listing every third-party dependency and its license, flagging anything copyleft."
- Privacy: "Trace where we touch personal data across the codebase into an artifact for the privacy review."
- Security: "Build an artifact of the auth findings from this review, each linked to the code."
- FinOps / platform finance: "Map our cloud resources from the Terraform into an artifact, grouped by service, with the big cost drivers."
- Software engineers: "Make an artifact walking through this PR — the diff, the reasoning, and what I tested."
- Designers & frontend engineers: "Give me an artifact with 5 UX variations of this signup form, built from our component library."
- Staff engineers & architects: "Map how the payments service fits together into an artifact, from the code."
- SRE & on-call: "Turn this incident into an artifact — timeline, suspect commits, error spike from our monitoring — and republish as I work through it."
- Engineering managers: "Build an artifact of what merged on my team this week from the PRs, grouped by project."

# concise-sales-email-draft

## Style rules
- Keep the email brief (generally 3–8 sentences).
- Match the sender's voice and preferred greeting/sign-off.
- Remove unnecessary hedging (e.g., "might", "perhaps") unless uncertainty is essential.
- Prefer concrete next steps and questions.
- Assume positive intent; keep tone professional.

## Output format
Subject: <short subject>

<email body>

Next steps:
- <1–3 bullets>

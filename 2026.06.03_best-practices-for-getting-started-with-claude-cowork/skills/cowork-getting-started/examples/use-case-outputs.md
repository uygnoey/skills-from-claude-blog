# Cowork use cases and expected outputs

## Daily briefing
- Inputs: Slack, Gmail
- Output: a short report with a TL;DR of what to look into, flagged emails grouped by type, channel summaries, and any overnight product-related incidents that could have impacted marketing.

## Budget pacing
- Inputs: Google Ads, Meta Ads
- Output: a live artifact (HTML dashboard) that pulls daily spend and calculates pacing.

## Reporting
- Inputs: Google Search Console
- Output: a single sheet and/or a report written in the desired template.

## What is this post?
This post announces Workspaces in the Anthropic API Console—separate environments that help teams manage multiple Claude deployments with clearer organization and controls.

## When is it useful?
Use Workspaces when you need to separate projects/environments (for example, staging vs. production), delegate access safely, or enforce different spend and rate limits per deployment.

## Key points
- Workspaces let you group related resources (API keys, usage, settings) under a single workspace.
- You can set monthly spend limits per workspace.
- You can configure workspace-specific rate limits (within your organization limits).
- You can manage user permissions at the workspace level.
- You can monitor usage and costs broken down by workspace.

## Bundled resources

- Guide: A step-by-step checklist for setting up workspace-scoped keys, limits, and access control.

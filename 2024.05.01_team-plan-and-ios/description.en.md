## What is this post?
This post announces two Claude product updates: the Claude Team plan (a paid workspace plan for teams) and the Claude iOS app (a free mobile app). 

## When is it useful?
- When you need higher per-user usage, admin/billing controls, and access to the full Claude 3 model family for a team.
- When you want to use Claude on mobile with chat sync and photo/file-based vision use cases.

## Key points
- Team plan includes increased usage, access to Opus/Sonnet/Haiku, a 200K context window, admin tools, and all Claude Pro features.
- Team plan pricing is $30 per user per month with a 5-seat minimum.
- The Claude iOS app is free for all plans and supports chat syncing and vision (photos/files).

## What is this post?
This post announces Claude’s Research capability (agentic, multi-step information gathering with citations) and a Google Workspace integration that lets Claude use your Gmail, Calendar, and Docs as additional context.

## When is it useful?
Use it when you need thorough, citation-backed answers that combine information from the web with your organization’s internal context (emails, docs, calendar), such as preparing for launches, customer meetings, or technical design decisions.

## Key points
- Research can conduct multiple searches and follow-up investigations to build a comprehensive answer with citations.
- Google Workspace integration lets Claude securely search Gmail, Calendar, and Docs to reduce manual context gathering.
- Enterprise features like document cataloging can improve retrieval quality when enabled by admins.

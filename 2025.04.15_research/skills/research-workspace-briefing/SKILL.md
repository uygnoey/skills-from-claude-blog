## Instructions
Use this skill when you need a comprehensive briefing with citations.

1. Clarify the research question and the decision it supports.
2. Identify the internal context to include (emails, docs, calendar events) via Google Workspace integration.
3. Gather external context from the web to fill gaps and validate assumptions.
4. Produce a structured briefing that clearly distinguishes internal findings vs. external findings.
5. Include citations so the reader can verify each key claim.

## Examples
- Create a client-meeting briefing by combining email threads and calendar notes with recent company updates from the web.
- Draft a launch plan by blending internal positioning docs with competitive research gathered from the web.

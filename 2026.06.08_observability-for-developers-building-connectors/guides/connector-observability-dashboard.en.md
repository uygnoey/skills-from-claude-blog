# Connector observability dashboard (guide)

## What is this?
A short guide to what you can learn from the connector observability dashboard for published connectors in the Claude directory.

## What you can measure
### Adoption
- Active users
- Total tool calls
- Directory rank over time

### Reliability and performance
- Health score
- Error rates
- Latency overview
- Per-tool error breakdown

### Usage by product surface
- Tool calls broken down by product (e.g., Claude, Claude Code, Cowork)

## Access requirements
- Available in public beta.
- Find it in Claude under **Directory** in **Organization settings**.
- Requires a Team or Enterprise account with Admin or Owner access, or an Enterprise custom role with the **Libraries** permission.

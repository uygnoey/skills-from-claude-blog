# Guide: AI-native startup stages (Idea → MVP → Launch → Scale)

## 概要
本ガイドは原文に基づき、実行しやすい形に要点を整理します。

## 適用方法
- Define your current stage (Idea, MVP, Launch, Scale) and write down the exit criteria you need to reach.
- Use Claude for structured thinking tasks (problem framing, competitive mapping, discovery planning) during the Idea stage.
- During MVP, use Claude Code to build safely: keep scope tight and put guardrails around security and architecture decisions.
- During Launch, replace founder attention with repeatable agentic workflows and measurement.
- During Scale, systematize what worked and expand tool usage with clear ownership and processes.

## チェックリスト
- Stage and exit criteria are written down.
- A plan exists for customer discovery and competitive landscape mapping.
- MVP codebase has minimal scope and basic security/architecture practices.
- Launch measurement distinguishes early hype from real product-market fit signals.
- Key recurring founder tasks are turned into repeatable workflows.

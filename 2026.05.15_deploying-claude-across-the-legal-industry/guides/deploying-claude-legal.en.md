# Deploying Claude across a legal organization (guide)

## Overview
This guide summarizes the post’s approach to deploying Claude in legal teams: selecting the right product for the work, customizing via connectors and practice-area plugins, and adopting in phases.

## Product selection (matrix)
- **Claude Chat**: quick research, Q&A, and lightweight drafting.
- **Claude Cowork**: matter-level work that spans multiple files and connected apps.
- **Claude for Microsoft 365**: work done primarily inside Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and Outlook.
- **Claude Platform**: build and deploy custom legal applications.

## Customize for legal workflows
- Use **connectors** to bring relevant internal systems and knowledge sources into Claude-supported workflows.
- Use **practice-area plugins** to reflect playbooks, precedent, and house style.

## Rollout in phases
Adopt Claude in phases and expand based on measured value in core workflows such as contract review/redlining, diligence, litigation preparation, and regulatory monitoring.

## What is this post?
This post explains how legal teams deploy Claude across common legal workflows, and how to choose the right Claude product for each type of work.

## When is it useful?
Use this when you’re rolling out Claude in a law firm or in-house legal department and need a practical adoption plan, along with examples of where legal teams get value (contracts, diligence, litigation prep, regulatory work).

## Key points
- Legal teams are increasingly adopting generative AI; the post cites a 2026 FTI Consulting / Relativity General Counsel Report showing rapid growth in reported usage.
- Teams that get ahead embed agentic AI into day-to-day practice rather than using it only for ad hoc chat.
- The post highlights a product “matrix” to decide when to use:
  - Claude Chat
  - Claude Cowork (matter-level work spanning files and apps)
  - Claude for Microsoft 365 (Word/Excel/PowerPoint/Outlook)
  - Claude Platform (custom applications)
- It describes connectors and practice-area plugins that tailor Claude to legal playbooks, precedent, and house style.

## Bundled resources
- A high-level product selection framework (“product matrix”).
- A three-phase adoption roadmap.
- Practice-area plugin catalog (12 plugins referenced in the post).

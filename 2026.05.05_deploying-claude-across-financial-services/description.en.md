# Deploying Claude across financial services

## What is this post?

Anthropic's May 5, 2026 announcement and accompanying deployment guide for financial services. The post frames how banks, asset managers, and insurers run Claude across research, deal work, underwriting, claims, model reviews, and month-end close, and points readers to a practical guide that covers the full Claude product surface (Chat, Cowork, Code, Microsoft 365 add-ins, the Claude Platform, and Claude Managed Agents), 10 pre-built finance agent templates, and a three-phase adoption playbook.

## When is it useful?

- Whenever an AI leader inside a financial services firm has to decide which Claude product belongs in which workflow.
- When packaging a Claude rollout plan for a regulated environment that needs phased deployment, governance, and customer references.
- When mapping the 10 pre-built finance agent templates (pitch builder, meeting preparer, earnings reviewer, model builder, market researcher, KYC screener, valuation reviewer, general ledger reconciler, month-end closer, statement auditor) onto an existing operating model.

## Key points

- Most financial services firms already run more than one Claude product side by side, and the deployment guide gives an explicit product matrix for Chat vs. Cowork vs. Code vs. Microsoft 365 add-ins vs. the Claude Platform vs. Claude Managed Agents.
- 10 pre-built finance agent templates ship as plugins in Claude Cowork and Claude Code, and as cookbooks for Claude Managed Agents — covering research and client coverage, credit/risk/compliance, and finance/operations.
- The guide bundles customer stories from AIG, Commonwealth Bank of Australia, IG Group, and Moody's.
- A three-phase adoption playbook (foundation, pilot, scale) gives the actions and pro-tips that work at each stage.
- Use cases are organized by sector across investment banking and private equity, wealth and asset management, and retail and commercial banking.

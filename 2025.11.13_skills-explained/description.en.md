## What is this post?
This post explains how Skills fit into the Claude ecosystem by comparing Skills with prompts, Projects, MCP, and subagents, and by showing how to combine them in workflows.

## When is it useful?
Use it when you’re deciding whether to encode repeated instructions as a Skill, keep them as ad-hoc prompts, place background knowledge into a Project, connect data with MCP, or delegate work to subagents.

## Key points
- Skills are reusable bundles (instructions + optional code/assets) that load dynamically, using progressive disclosure.
- Prompts are conversational and one-off.
- Projects are persistent workspaces with their own knowledge bases.
- Subagents are specialized assistants with their own prompts and tool permissions.
- MCP connects Claude to external systems where data lives.

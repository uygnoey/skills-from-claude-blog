# Introducing the Max Plan

## What is this post?
This post introduces Claude's Max plan, which provides higher usage limits and priority access to new features.

## When is it useful?
Useful if you need to decide whether higher limits or plan changes are justified by your daily workload and deadlines.

## Key points
- Max targets users who collaborate with Claude heavily and need higher limits.
- It supports extended conversations, large documents, complex data work, and frequent daily use.
- It offers multiple levels and has ongoing upgrades over time (see the update section in the post).

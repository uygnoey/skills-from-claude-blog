# Example: Onboarding brief request

Create an onboarding brief for a new team member joining [Team].

Search across:
- Project specifications and strategic plans in SharePoint/OneDrive
- Recent decision threads in Outlook
- Key Teams discussions and meeting summaries

Deliver:
- 1-page overview of the project goals and current status
- Recent decisions and why they were made
- Who to talk to for each domain area (based on discussion patterns)
- Pointers to the most important documents and where they live

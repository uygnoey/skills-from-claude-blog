## What is this post?
This post introduces product updates that position Claude as a “thinking partner,” including memory, voice, file creation, and deeper ways to work across devices and connected tools.

## When is it useful?
It’s useful when you want Claude to stay with you across iterations—remembering preferences, brainstorming by voice, and turning ideas into concrete deliverables like documents, spreadsheets, presentations, or small prototypes.

## Key points
- Memory can retain preferences, projects, and working style across conversations.
- Voice mode supports more natural back-and-forth, especially on mobile.
- Claude can help you work across contexts (mobile, browser, connected tools).
- Claude can create and refine artifacts (documents, spreadsheets, presentations, prototypes) through iteration.

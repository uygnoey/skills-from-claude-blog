# Rolling out agentic AI in the enterprise (summary guide)

## What is this guide?
A concise, implementation-oriented summary of the post’s framework: three pillars of enterprise AI transformation plus a six-month rollout framing.

## When is it useful?
When you need a shared language for stakeholders (leaders, IT, security, and front-line teams) to align on how to roll out agents beyond isolated pilots.

## Key points
- Use the three pillars (enablement, process compression with human judgement, product capabilities) as your decision filter.
- Treat rollout as a phased deployment with ownership, governance, and reuse of institutional knowledge.
- Use narratives and internal case studies to drive adoption.

## What is this post?
A high-level guide to deploying “agentic AI” inside large organizations, organized around three pillars of enterprise AI transformation and illustrated with customer stories (L’Oréal, Lyft, Rakuten).

## When is it useful?
When you need an executive-level framework for rolling out agents across teams (not a single prototype), including upskilling, process compression with human oversight, and building new AI-powered product capabilities.

## Key points
- Enterprises that see compounding returns embed agents into day-to-day workflows, encode institutional knowledge into systems, and invest in rollout rather than isolated experiments.
- The post proposes three pillars of enterprise AI transformation and positions them as a way to avoid deployments that stall after early incremental gains.
- It includes a six-month deployment framework for rolling out Claude Cowork.

- None (the post is narrative guidance; no code, templates, or hook examples are provided).

# Partner catalog — verbatim from the post

This reference mirrors the partners, offerings, claims, and quotes in "How our partners are putting Opus to work for cybersecurity." It is faithful to the source post and does not add capabilities the post does not describe.

## Bucket A — continuous offensive testing at production scale

### Wiz Red Agent

- AI-powered attacker that uses Opus to reason like a human pentester across production web applications and APIs.
- Analyzes application logic, chains steps, and adapts to real-time server responses to surface logic-driven flaws traditional scanners miss.
- Runs continuously across **more than 150,000 production assets a week**.
- Surfaces **thousands of validated high- and critical-severity findings weekly with zero false positives** in customer production.
- Each finding is validated with proof of exploitability and business context from the Wiz Security Graph.
- Quote — Alon Schindel, VP AI & Threat Research, Wiz: *"Security teams are no longer limited by a lack of data, but by the ability to act on it. By embedding frontier models into Wiz Agents, we're enabling organizations to defend at the speed of AI."*

### Palo Alto Networks Unit 42 Frontier AI Defense

- Expert-led service that uses Opus to find hidden vulnerabilities, map how they chain into critical attack paths, and build a roadmap for hardening against AI-enabled attacks.
- Pairs exposure analysis with a benchmarked blueprint for machine-speed defense and hands-on transformation work.
- Internal testing claim: **the equivalent of a year's worth of penetration testing effort completed in under three weeks**.
- Quote — Sam Rubin, SVP of Unit 42, Palo Alto Networks: *"As attackers weaponize frontier models to automate cyberattacks, the defense must move faster."*

### CrowdStrike Frontier AI Readiness and Resilience Service

- Pairs Opus with CrowdStrike's **AI Red Team Services** and proprietary agent frameworks.
- Continuously hunts for latent zero-days in customer applications, validates findings, and accelerates remediation before new code reaches production.
- Brings the capability to a platform trusted by **more than 60% of the Fortune 500**.
- Quote — Mark Manglicmot, Global VP of Consulting Services, CrowdStrike: *"Frontier models like Anthropic's Claude Opus are giving defenders a capability advantage that didn't exist a year ago, pushing vulnerability management all the way to the left."*

## Bucket B — closing the gap between finding and fixing

### Accenture Cyber.AI

- Agentic platform that connects assets, identities, threats, and controls into a single operational model that Opus reasons across.
- Runs detection, prioritization, and remediation as a continuous loop.
- Validated at scale on Accenture's own infrastructure first:
  - Security testing coverage from **roughly 10% to over 80%** across **1,600 applications and 500,000+ APIs**.
  - Scan turnaround cut from **3–5 days to under an hour**.
- Quote — Harpreet Sidhu, Global Lead, Accenture Cybersecurity: *"Business leaders are navigating the fastest moving and most complex cyber threat landscape in history. We're partnering with Anthropic to deliver the tools clients need to stay ahead."*

### TrendAI Vision One

- Uses Opus-assisted vulnerability research to help enterprises across **185 countries** identify exposure and mitigate risk through virtual patching.
- Validated findings also flow into the **TrendAI Zero Day Initiative** for coordinated disclosure.
- Protects at-risk systems **up to 96 days before a vendor patch** is available.
- Quote — Rachel Jin, Chief Platform and Business Officer, Head of TrendAI: *"As AI accelerates vulnerability discovery, the real challenge for defenders becomes remediation at scale. Together with Anthropic, we're helping customers reduce risk through mitigation and virtual patching before attackers can exploit the gap."*

### Deloitte Continuous Threat Exposure Management (CTEM) on Deloitte Ascend

- Runs discovery, validation, prioritization, and remediation as one workflow, including countermeasure design when no patch exists.
- Opus's code reasoning and automated stability testing gives teams confidence to remediate in **hours rather than days or weeks**.
- Quote — Adnan Amjad, partner and US Cyber leader, Deloitte: *"CTEM built on Ascend exists to help reduce decision latency in vulnerability remediation; the gap helps determine whether attackers or defenders win the window."*

## Bucket C — getting AI into production, governed

### PwC Claude Native Cybersecurity

- Two components:
  - **Secure AI Adoption** — moves enterprises from sandbox to production in **weeks rather than quarters**, with the deployment, governance, and audit evidence that helps the CISO and CRO bring innovation to their teams with confidence.
  - **Scaled Frontier Defense** — integrates Opus-powered agentic reasoning into existing vulnerability management, detection, security engineering, and GRC workflows, enabling autonomous execution within defined guardrails and auditability.
- Quote — Morgan Adamski, U.S. Cyber, Data & Tech Leader, PwC: *"This is a defining moment for cybersecurity, where AI-driven transformation becomes essential to staying resilient and competitive."*

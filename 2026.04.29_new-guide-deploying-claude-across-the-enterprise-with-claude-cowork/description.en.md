# New guide: Deploying Claude across the enterprise with Claude Cowork

## What is this post?

Anthropic's April 29, 2026 announcement introduces a new guide for deploying Claude Cowork — the desktop counterpart that brings agent capability to analysts, lawyers, account executives, marketers, and other non-developer roles — across an enterprise. The guide draws on patterns from Anthropic's own teams plus customers like Thomson Reuters, Zapier, and Jamf, and covers how to start, structure a pilot, drive adoption, and reach organization-wide deployment.

## When useful

- You are responsible for rolling out Claude Cowork to a business function (analysts, legal, sales, marketing, etc.) and need a structured plan rather than an ad-hoc pilot.
- You need to choose a first use case and structure a pilot evaluation that can credibly justify expansion.
- You want a six-month roadmap that moves from champion teams to organization-wide deployment.
- You want concrete reference patterns from Anthropic's own finance, legal, sales, and product teams plus customer stories from Thomson Reuters, Zapier, and Jamf.

## Key points

- Claude Cowork meets work where it already lives: local files and folders, connected apps like Slack and Google Drive, and the browser.
- Paired with Claude for Excel and Claude for PowerPoint, it carries context across spreadsheets and slide decks in a single workflow.
- Plugins, skills, and commands let teams customize Claude to their work environment.
- The guide includes a five-level maturity model for Claude Cowork adoption — from chat Q&A all the way to department-wide plugins.
- It covers how to choose your first use case and structure a pilot evaluation.
- It provides a six-month roadmap moving from champion teams to organization-wide deployment.
- It documents how Anthropic's finance, legal, sales, and product teams use Cowork in production.
- Customer stories: Thomson Reuters, Zapier, and Jamf.
